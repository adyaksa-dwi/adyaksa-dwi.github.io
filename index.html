Lumina Flux

Lumina Flux, the beauty of the name is in how the two words, Lumina and Flux, combine to create a new, powerful meaning that perfectly describes your skills. "Lumina" is a word rooted in Latin, and it means light. "Flux" means a continuous change or flow. Think of a river in a state of constant motion. When you put them together, Lumina Flux means "the flow of light". It doesn't just describe what you do; it describes how you do it—with a sense of motion, change, and creativity that flows like light itself.
